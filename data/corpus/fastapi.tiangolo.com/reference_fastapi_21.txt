Title: FastAPI Additional Responses and Deprecation
URL: https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/reference/fastapi/
Summary: This section describes how to define additional responses in FastAPI that will be included in the generated OpenAPI documentation. It highlights the ability to mark path operations as deprecated, which will also be reflected in the OpenAPI output. Examples and links to further resources on additional responses and structuring larger applications with API routers are provided.
