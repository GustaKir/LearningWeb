Title: Translations Added and Updated
URL: https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/release-notes/
Summary: This section highlights recent contributions to the FastAPI documentation, including new translations in Spanish, Portuguese, and Turkish, as well as a fix in the German translation. Noteworthy PRs include those by contributors @camigomezdev, @lucasbalieiro, @anitahammer, @Joao-Pedro-P-Holanda, and @hasansezertasan.
