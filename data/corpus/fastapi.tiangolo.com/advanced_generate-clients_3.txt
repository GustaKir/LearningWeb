Title: Generating Clients in FastAPI
URL: https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/advanced/generate-clients/
Summary: This document discusses how to generate clients in FastAPI, including customization options like custom operation IDs, unique ID functions, and preprocessing the OpenAPI specification for the client generator. It also highlights the benefits of generating TypeScript clients with these customizations.
