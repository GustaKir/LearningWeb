Title: Translations in FastAPI Release Notes
URL: https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/release-notes/
Summary: This section highlights the recent additions of translations for various FastAPI tutorials and documentation. Notably, it includes Japanese translations for the Advanced Tutorial, security steps, query parameters, and CORS, as well as a Portuguese translation for External Links.
