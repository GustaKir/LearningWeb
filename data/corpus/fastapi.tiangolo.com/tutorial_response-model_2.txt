Title: Response Models in FastAPI

Summary: This section covers various aspects of using response models in FastAPI, including parameters like `response_model_include` and `response_model_exclude`, data handling for fields with defaults, and options for excluding unset fields. It also highlights the use of lists versus sets and provides a recap of the key points.
