Title: Release Notes - Other Changes
URL: https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/quick-reference/release-notes/2024
Summary: This section outlines various improvements and updates in the Streamlit framework, including enhancements to WebSocket efficiency, updates to the `streamlit hello` app with Google Material icons, and improved type hinting for several components such as `st.number_input` and `st.feedback`. Additionally, theme management for embedded apps has been enhanced.
