Title: Streamlit Version 1.34.0 Release Notes
URL: https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/quick-reference/release-notes/2024
Summary: Released on May 2, 2024, Version 1.34.0 introduces `st.experimental_dialog` for creating independent modal overlays. Notable changes include support for Google Material Symbols in various functions, background colors in Markdown, and autoplay features for audio and video components.
