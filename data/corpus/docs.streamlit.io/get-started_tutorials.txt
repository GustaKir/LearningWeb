# [](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/tutorials#first-steps-building-streamlit-apps)First steps building Streamlit apps
If you've just read through our [Basic concepts](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/fundamentals/main-concepts) and want to get your hands on Streamlit. Check out these tutorials. Make sure you have [installed Streamlit](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/installation) so you can execute the code yourself.
[_description_](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/tutorials/create-an-app)
[Create an app](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/tutorials/create-an-app) uses the concepts learned in Fundamentals along with caching to walk through making your first app.
[_auto_stories_](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/tutorials/create-a-multipage-app)
[Create a multipage app](https://docs.streamlit.io/get-started/tutorials/create-a-multipage-app) walks through the easy steps to add pages to your app.
