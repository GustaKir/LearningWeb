Title: Streamlit 2025 Release Notes: Other Changes
URL: https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/quick-reference/release-notes/2025
Summary: This section highlights various updates in Streamlit 2025, including the ability for users to hide dataframe columns, change formatting for numbers, dates, and times, and auto-size column widths. It also notes the addition of support for Polars dataframe hashing, removal of 'rich' as a required dependency, an improved display for 'st.file_uploader' in narrow containers, and a bug fix to prevent tabs from flickering.
