# [](https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/tutorials/databases#connect-streamlit-to-data-sources)Connect Streamlit to data sources
These step-by-step guides demonstrate how to connect Streamlit apps to various databases & APIs. They use Streamlit's [Secrets management](https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/concepts/connections/secrets-management) and [caching](https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/concepts/architecture/caching) to provide secure and fast data access.
