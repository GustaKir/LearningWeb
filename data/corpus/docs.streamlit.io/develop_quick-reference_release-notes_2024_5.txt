Title: Streamlit 2024 Release Notes: Other Changes
URL: https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/quick-reference/release-notes/2024
Summary: This section outlines various updates in the Streamlit 2024 release, including the addition of HTML class prefixes for widget keys, the introduction of more specific exceptions in the `StreamlitAPIException` class, full-screen toggle capabilities for `st.map` and `st.pydeck_chart`, upgrades to frontend dependencies for Vega, and compatibility updates with Watchdog and Tenacity versions.
