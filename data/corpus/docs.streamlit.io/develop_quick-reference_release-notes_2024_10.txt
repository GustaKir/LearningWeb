Title: Streamlit Release Notes 2024
URL: https://docs.streamlit.io/develop/quick-reference/release-notes/2024
Summary: This document outlines notable changes in Streamlit 2024, including new features such as horizontal rendering for `st.bar_chart`, icon support for `st.expander`, vertical alignment options in `st.columns`, and enhanced custom component callback functionality. Additionally, it notes the discontinuation of widget rendering outside their main body in fragments and improvements in axis label customization for various chart types.
