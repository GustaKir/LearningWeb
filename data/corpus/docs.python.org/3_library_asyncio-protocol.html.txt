Title: Transports and Protocols in Asyncio
URL: https://docs.python.org/3/library/asyncio-protocol.html
Summary: This document discusses the asyncio library in Python, focusing on the concepts of transports and protocols. It outlines the hierarchy of transports, including base, read-only, and write-only transports, providing essential information for understanding how asynchronous communication is managed in Python.
