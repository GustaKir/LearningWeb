Title: Pending Removals in Python 3.13
URL: https://docs.python.org/3/whatsnew/3.12.html
Summary: This section lists modules and APIs scheduled for removal in Python 3.13, including modules such as `aifc`, `cgi`, and `telnetlib`. It also mentions the removal of `lib2to3` and the **2to3** program, along with specific APIs like `configparser.LegacyInterpolation` and `locale.resetlocale()`, among others.
