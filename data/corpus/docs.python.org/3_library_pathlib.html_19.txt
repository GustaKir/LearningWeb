Title: Pathlib vs. Os.path: Corresponding Tools
URL: https://docs.python.org/3/library/pathlib.html
Summary: This section explains that pathlib is not a direct replacement for os.path and provides a table mapping os functions to their pathlib equivalents, highlighting the differences and tools available in both libraries.
---

As a consequence of these differences, pathlib is not a drop-in replacement for [`os.path`](https://docs.python.org/3/library/os.path.html#module-os.path "os.path: Operations on pathnames.").
